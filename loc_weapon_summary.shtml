Melee weapons are "hit prioritised" from top to bottom. Note that the Stun Rod will override all hit priorities as long as it makes contact.

Melee weapon damage values are "per contact unit of time" which is a factor of 40 per second of contact, with the maximum possible deployment time being 3 seconds. Maximum values are given below:
