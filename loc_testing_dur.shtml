Vertical Tank Platform and Leg Durability

Vertical Tank durability ratings are based on armour points, which are equavalent to damage points inflicted by weaponry. The tables below detail the exact number of durability points for each VT available, as well as the leg specific durability (for capture damage estimation purposes).

Based on this information, the "durability rating points" given in each individual VT Profile are equivalent to 600 armour points, the exact damage done by a 430-SB round. All other armour ratings (and the necessary damage to destroy a given VT) may be extrapolated from this data.
